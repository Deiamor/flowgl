# Case studies

Public references for flowgl deployments. Maintainers add entries here
as Show & Tell threads on [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Deiamor/flowgl/discussions/categories/show-and-tell)
mature. 1.0 readiness wants ≥ 3 production references; that's the
bar for "real-world traction" in the [SEMVER.md](./SEMVER.md) 1.0 plan.

## How to add your project

1. Open a Show & Tell discussion with screenshots, scale (nodes, edges,
   typical view), the renderer (`webgl2` / `canvas2d`), and any
   custom node-types you registered.
2. If you'd like the project linked here, ping a maintainer in the
   thread — we'll PR an entry after a brief review.
3. Anonymous (sector + scale only, no company name) is welcome.
