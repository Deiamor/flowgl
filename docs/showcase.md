## How listings are curated

The first column is the project, the second is what's notable about its
use of flowgl. We don't gatekeep — open-source, commercial, internal
tools, hobby projects all welcome. We do ask that the link works and
that the description is something you'd want a maintainer to read.

If a listing goes dead (link broken, project archived), we'll move it to
an "Inactive" section rather than delete it. Effort spent on the
ecosystem deserves to stay visible.
